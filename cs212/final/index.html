Revenge of Lights Out

Addendum:

During the development of the Lights Out game, a strategy was employed to create a random but solvable starting configuration. The challenge was to ensure that the puzzle could be solved by the player, creating a satisfying and engaging experience.

Process and Creative Choices:

  • Random Solvable Board: The initial configuration is generated by simulating random clicks on the board. This approach guarantees a solvable puzzle while providing a unique experience for each game session.

Challenges Faced:

  • Solvable Configuration: Ensuring that the randomly generated board is solvable required careful consideration of the toggling logic. The challenge was to strike a balance between randomness and solvability.

How Challenges Were Overcome:

  • Iterative Testing: The logic for random clicks was refined through iterative testing to verify that the generated configurations were consistently solvable. Adjustments were made to improve the algorithm.

Insights Gained:

  • User Satisfaction: The introduction of a random but solvable configuration enhances user satisfaction by providing a dynamic and challenging puzzle for each playthrough. This contributes to the overall enjoyment of the game.
